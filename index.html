// How It Works

From data to action
in real time.

SafeFrame monitors behavioral patterns — not biometrics — to surface risks before they escalate, giving your staff the context they need to respond with confidence.

01 / DETECT
📡

Behavior-Based Detection

SafeFrame analyzes behavioral patterns using data your district already has — no new cameras, no audio, and no biometric tracking required.

02 / ALERT
🔔

Intelligent Triage

Alerts are ranked by severity and routed directly to the right staff member — counselor, administrator, or security — with full context included.

03 / RESPOND
🤝

Coordinated Response

Staff acknowledge, act, and document within one unified platform. Every response is logged for compliance, reporting, and continuous improvement.

Not to surveil them.

OUR PRIVACY COMMITMENTS

FERPA compliant architecture✓

SafeFrame was architected with student privacy as a non-negotiable core principle. We believe effective school safety and strong civil liberties are not in conflict — they reinforce each other.

Our behavioral analysis operates exclusively on structured data that districts already maintain. No new cameras. No microphones. No biometrics. Ever.

  • No facial recognition or biometric tracking
  • No audio or video surveillance required
  • Full FERPA compliance built into every layer
  • Data never sold or shared with third parties
  • Regular third-party security audits
  • Transparent data practices with district oversight
