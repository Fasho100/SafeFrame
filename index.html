// How It Works

From data to action
in real time.

SafeFrame monitors behavioral patterns — not biometrics — to surface risks before they escalate, giving your staff the context they need to respond with confidence.

01 / DETECT
📡

Behavior-Based Detection

SafeFrame analyzes behavioral patterns using data your district already has — no new cameras, no audio, and no biometric tracking required.

02 / ALERT
🔔

Intelligent Triage

Alerts are ranked by severity and routed directly to the right staff member — counselor, administrator, or security — with full situational context included.

03 / RESPOND
🤝

Coordinated Response

Staff acknowledge, act, and document within one unified platform. Every response is logged for compliance, reporting, and continuous improvement.
